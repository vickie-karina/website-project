A little bit about me.

I'm from Boston, Massachusetts and currently live in Stamford, CT. My journey into tech started with a degree in graphic design from Northeastern University. After taking a couple of coding classes in college and being a part of the workforce for a couple of years, I became more and more interested in front-end development and learning how to code.

That curiosity led me to sign up for intensive courses on Skillcrush.com, where I am currently taking classes while still working in design full-time. I believe that learning how to code will open me up to new opportunities and make me a more well-rounded designer. My goal is to ulitmately make a switch to becoming a full-time front-end developer.
